Series format

Every legend feature follows the same structure so you can reuse it in your classroom, meetup, or mentorship program.

Long-form interviews

45-minute conversations transcribed and edited for clarity. Includes actionable advice and resources.

Artifacts & archives

We collect screenshots, slide decks, and demos from each legend so future builders can learn.

Community premiere

Each story drops with a live community call, Q&A, and a builder challenge inspired by the legend.

Nominate a legend

Know someone we should feature? Email hello@trailblazerscholarship.com with their story, links to their work, and how they’ve lifted Arkansas builders.

What we look for

  • Documented projects or programs that improved access to tech, data, or automation.
  • Evidence of mentorship or teaching others.
  • Willingness to share lessons learned and challenges faced.

How the process works

We review nominations monthly, schedule a 30-minute discovery call, and co-write the final story with the legend.
