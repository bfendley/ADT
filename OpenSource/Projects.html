How to contribute

We keep the contribution process clear so you can focus on the code, docs, or design.

1

Pick an issue

Check the help-wanted or good-first-issue labels. Comment to claim it and we’ll add you as an assignee.

2

Pair with mentors

Join office hours or book a 30-minute pairing slot. We’ll help with setup, scoping, and reviews.

3

Open a PR

Ship your changes with screenshots, tests, and notes on what you learned. We merge fast and celebrate publicly.

Join the maintainer circle

Email hello@trailblazerscholarship.com if you’d like to become a maintainer or host a community lab.

What you’ll get

Dedicated Slack channel, access to staging environments, and quarterly co-maintainer summits.

What we ask

Ship at least one release or workshop per quarter, document your process, and mentor a new contributor.
