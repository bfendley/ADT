Academy FAQ

How do I apply for financial aid?

Submit the combined scholarship and micro-grant form. Select “Academy fee waiver” and include the courses you plan to take.

Do I need to be in Arkansas?

Most modules are remote-friendly. Arkansas residents receive priority for live cohort seats.

Can employers sponsor a cohort?

Yes. Email academy@ardigitaltrailblazer.com to design a custom in-house sprint.
