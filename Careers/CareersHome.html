How we work together

Our process is lightweight and collaborative. We want you to know exactly how decisions are made and how your work will be used.

1

Intro call

Share your work samples and availability. We’ll walk through project goals and expectations.

2

Paid project sprint

Complete a scoped sprint with Trailblazer mentors. We pay for your time and ship something useful.

3

Feedback & decision

We review the sprint together, share feedback, and align on next steps—ongoing work, referrals, or community collaborations.

Why partners like working with us

We keep things simple so you can focus on the work and the impact.

Transparent scopes

Every engagement kicks off with a written brief, deliverables checklist, and communication cadence.

Community exposure

Your work is highlighted in the Trailblazer newsletter, legends series, and open source repos.

Shared resources

Access to internal tooling, templates, and our mentor network to stay supported.

Introduce yourself

Email hello@trailblazerscholarship.com with links to your work, availability, and the kind of projects you love.

What to include

  • Two recent projects or case studies.
  • Your preferred tools and focus areas.
  • When you’re available to start.

Response timeline

We reply within five business days. If it’s a fit, we’ll schedule an intro call and share the sprint brief.
