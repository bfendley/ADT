Why Work With Us

Ship > Slideware

Small scopes, fast feedback, real users. Demo weekly and see impact immediately.

Flexible & Remote

Most roles are part-time/contract and remote-friendly. We plan async, meet when needed.

Community First

We invest in Arkansas—scholarships, Academy, and open-source. Your work helps neighbors.

Hiring Process

1) Quick Intro (15–20 min)

Tell us what you’ve shipped. We share context and scope. No trick questions.

2) Paid Trial or Take-home

Tiny, scoped task aligned to the actual role. We pay for meaningful time.

3) Portfolio / Code Review

Walk us through your code or artifacts. Focus on decisions, tradeoffs, and results.

4) Offer & Start

Clear scope, rate, cadence, and deliverables. We move fast.

How to Apply

Send One Email

Email careers@ardigitaltrailblazer.com with a short note on why the role fits and links to work you’re proud of.

  • Attach or link to a resume/portfolio
  • Share availability for a 20-minute chat
  • Let us know your preferred hourly rate or salary range

Application Form

We respond within five business days. No recruiters, please.
