Mission & invitation

Arkansas Digital Trailblazer helps local builders launch projects, unlock scholarships, and stay in Arkansas with meaningful work. We combine hands-on training, real-world deliverables, and a tight-knit community so technologists can grow without leaving home.

We’re building programs that make it easy to learn, ship, and share—from classroom lesson plans to open source projects and startup experiments. If you care about creating opportunity for Arkansas talent, there’s a seat for you at our table.

How to join us

We’re looking for mentors, donors, open source contributors, storytellers, and partners who want to invest in Arkansas talent.

  • Coach workshops or host a Saturday lab.
  • Fund scholarships, micro-grants, or classroom pilots.
  • Contribute code, curriculum, or case studies for the community.
