Our mission

Building more Arkansas builders

Arkansas Digital Trailblazer is a grassroots effort to help technologists, educators, and founders learn by doing. We publish everything we build so the next neighbor can start further ahead.

Free programs Hands-on mentorship Documented playbooks

What drives us

We believe Arkansas has all the talent it needs—when pathways are visible, resources are shared, and knowledge is open.

Our work centers on three outcomes: access to skills, access to tools, and a community that celebrates shipping.

Where we focus our energy

From high school classrooms to civic data projects, we lean into the places where an extra push unlocks outsized impact.

Skill pathways

Trailblazer Academy sessions cover Workday, automation, data, and public storytelling so builders can move from idea to shipped artifact.

Funding access

Scholarships and micro-grants remove the blockers—software licenses, substitute teachers, prototyping materials, and travel.

Open collaboration

We maintain repos, documentation, and community calls that make it easy to plug in, remix, and teach others.

Guiding principles

These are the filters we run every program through. If a project doesn’t fit, we work with partners who can pick it up.

Build in public

Every workshop ends with published notes, repos, or Looms so other Arkansans can reuse the work.

Design for teachers & teams

Our resources work in real classrooms, operations teams, and civic offices—not just slides.

Respect time & budgets

We scope sprints tightly, answer quickly, and publish ready-to-use templates.

Celebrate local wins

We spotlight Arkansas legends so new builders see what’s possible close to home.
