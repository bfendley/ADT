Our mission

Building More Arkansas Builders

We help Arkansans access skills, funding, and mentorship so they can ship projects that matter in their hometowns.

What We Focus On

  • Practical training with real deliverables.
  • Accessible funding to remove blockers.
  • Community storytelling to inspire the next cohort.

Values

Ship Small, Ship Often

We help people take the next small step—launch a prototype, publish a Loom, share a reflection.

Community Ownership

We build with partners statewide so that programs are co-owned and sustainable.

Generosity & Accountability

We operate with transparency, pay people for their time, and expect follow-through.

Timeline

2022

Launched the first Trailblazer Scholarship and awarded three builders from Little Rock, Fayetteville, and Pine Bluff.

2023

Expanded Academy pilots, hosted Workday weekends, and rolled out the Automation Cookbook.

2024

Opened micro-grants and documented the first cohort of Trailblazer Legends.

Team

Founding Team

A crew of Workday practitioners, automation engineers, and educators with roots across Arkansas.

Community Mentors

20+ mentors supporting academy cohorts, micro-grant projects, and scholarship recipients.
