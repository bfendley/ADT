Mission

Bryan Fendley

Growing up, it seemed a common goal was to get out of Arkansas to get a good career. This is apparent when in 2017 I was taking a programming course, the sole one offered by my school After using this to nearly a decade of career, I have finally come to the conclusion that local talent must be given an opportunity to shine, and experiment in order to succeed in such an intense global market. That is why I've start Arkansas Digital Trailblazers, where we can ensure our state continues to produce and hold talented individuals in the technical fields.
