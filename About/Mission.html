Our mission

Building More Arkansas Builders

We help Arkansans access skills, funding, and mentorship so they can ship projects that matter in their hometowns.

See how to get involved Explore the Academy

How we build a stronger digital Arkansas

We invest in the people, projects, and partnerships that help talent stay and thrive in Arkansas.

Real-world skill building

Hands-on projects, Workday integrations, and data challenges help learners master tools that employers need right now.

Mentorship that multiplies

We pair aspiring builders with experienced mentors who coach, review work, and open doors across the state.

Access to funding

Through grants, sponsorships, and local partners we lower the barrier to shipping ideas from Arkansas for Arkansas.

Meet the founder

Arkansas Digital Trailblazer is led by Bryan Fendley, a lifelong Arkansan investing in hometown builders.

Bryan Fendley

Founder · Workday Integration Developer · Monticello, Arkansas

Bryan is a Workday integration developer and data-quality advocate who started Arkansas Digital Trailblazer to help students and professionals launch real projects. He’s hands-on with Workday Studio/Extend, automation, and dashboards, and mentors learners breaking into ERP, data, and software careers.

“I believe Arkansans can build world-class projects without leaving home when they’re equipped with the right mentors and support.”
