FAQ

Questions We Hear Often

Transparency matters. Here are quick answers about funding, academy access, and how to partner with us.

How do I get started?

Start with the home page to explore scholarships, academy modules, and open-source projects. Then join the newsletter for monthly updates.

Is everything remote?

Most programs are remote-friendly. We also host in-person builder days and pop-up labs across the state.

How are funds distributed?

Scholarships and micro-grants are distributed via ACH or check within two weeks of award notification.

Can companies partner with you?

Yes! Email hello@trailblazerscholarship.com to sponsor a cohort, host a build day, or provide mentors.
