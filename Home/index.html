How Trailblazer support works

Every builder moves through a repeatable cycle—scope a problem, ship an artifact, and share the lessons so the next neighbor can build faster.

1

Scope & match

Meet with mentors to align on the outcome you need—course, grant, or collaboration.

2

Build together

Join live labs or async sprints with templates, code snippets, and fast feedback loops.

3

Document & demo

Capture Looms, publish briefs, and prep a showcase so others can reuse your work.

4

Share it forward

Mentor the next cohort, contribute to the toolkit, or host a local meetup.
