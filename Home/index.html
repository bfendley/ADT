Scholarships & Start Up Funding

Trailblazer Scholarship

$1,000 award for Arkansans building in code, data, or systems. Emphasis on initiative and community impact.

  • Open to HS seniors, college, and non-traditional learners
  • Short project story + links (GitHub, demo, screenshots)
  • Mentorship & portfolio support included

FAQ

Who can apply?

Arkansas residents or students at Arkansas schools pursuing technology, data, or digital innovation. Non-traditional learners are welcome.

Do you offer financial aid for paid courses?

Yes — use the scholarship & micro-grant application to request an Academy fee waiver.

Do I need perfect grades?

No. We evaluate curiosity, effort, and building. GPA is one signal, not the whole story.
