What you’ll receive

  • Tuition coverage for Trailblazer Academy workshops.
  • Micro-grants to outfit classrooms, prototypes, or team tools.
  • Mentorship from Arkansas coaches and open-source maintainers.

Who should apply

  • Students and educators launching hands-on tech projects.
  • Startup founders shaping their first product or pitch.
  • Community teams bringing digital ideas to Arkansas neighbors.

How it works

  1. Apply once for scholarship and micro-grant consideration.
  2. Match with an academy track or open-source build that fits.
  3. Ship your project with mentors, then showcase it statewide.
