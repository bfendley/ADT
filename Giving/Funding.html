Scholarships & Micro-Grants

Funding that meets you where you are

Trailblazer funding supports Arkansas builders with the tools, time, and mentorship needed to finish real projects. Awards are always free and include support from our mentor network.

How funding works

Apply once and choose the track that fits your project. We review applications bi-weekly and provide actionable feedback even if we can’t fund you yet.

Scholarships

Perfect for students and independent learners building toward a credential, portfolio piece, or classroom impact.

Trailblazer Scholarship

Designed for high school seniors, college students, and non-traditional learners pursuing technology, automation, or data projects.

What we look for

  • A project or artifact you built: code, automation, dashboard, documentation, or research.
  • A short story of the problem you tackled, your approach, the impact, and what you learned.
  • A plan for how you'll share knowledge or mentor others in the community.

What you receive

  • $1,000 award with an optional $250 equipment stipend.
  • Mentorship, resume and portfolio feedback, plus a mock interview.
  • Spotlight feature in the Trailblazer Legends series.

Funds can be used for tuition, certification fees, hardware, travel, or launch costs. Academy participants needing fee waivers can indicate that in the application.

Micro-grants

Built for builders, founders, and community organizers who need a push to prototype, ship, or share something with Arkansas.

Trailblazer Micro-Grants

Flexible funding that meets your project where it is. Pick the track that aligns with your next milestone.

  • Idea Validation: $250–$400 for domains, prototyping tools, user interviews, or early research.
  • Build Sprint: $500–$750 to ship an MVP, automation, dataset, or workflow that serves an Arkansas team.
  • Community Impact: $500–$1,000 to host events, workshops, or open-source contributions that grow local talent.

All micro-grant recipients share a short public update and schedule a 30-minute knowledge share with the community.

Application timeline

We keep the process simple so you can focus on building.

1

Submit your story

Email your project overview, timeline, and what you’ll ship. Include links or artifacts if you have them.

2

Review call

We hop on a 20-minute call to understand your goals, budget, and support needs.

3

Decision & support

Within two weeks you’ll receive funding details or recommendations for alternative support.

What’s included with funding

Funding comes with more than just dollars—we stick with you until the project ships.

Mentor pairing

Get matched with an expert who checks in weekly and helps unblock challenges.

Tool stipends

Need extra software, data, or hardware? We’ll work with you to cover essentials.

Public spotlight

Share your story in the Trailblazer newsletter, legends series, and open source repos.

Apply for funding

Send an email to hello@trailblazerscholarship.com with the track you’re interested in, your timeline, and how funding will be used.

What to include

  • Project overview and the Arkansas community it serves.
  • Budget or resources needed.
  • Links to demos, repos, or docs (if available).

Request our short application template or schedule a 15-minute prep call with a mentor.
