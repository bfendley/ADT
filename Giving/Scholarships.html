Trailblazer Scholarship

Who It's For

High school seniors, college students, and non-traditional learners in Arkansas pursuing technology, automation, or data projects.

  • Show us something you built (code, dashboard, automation, doc).
  • Tell the story: problem, approach, impact, and what you learned.
  • Share how you'll pay it forward in the community.

What You Get

  • $1,000 award + optional $250 equipment stipend.
  • Mentorship, resume/portfolio support, and a mock interview.
  • Feature in the Trailblazer Legends series.

Funding can be used for tuition, tools, travel, or launch costs.

Micro-Grants for Startups & Community Projects

Idea Validation

$250–$400 to cover initial experiments: domains, prototyping tools, or user interviews.

Build Sprint

$500–$750 to ship an MVP, automation, or dataset that serves an Arkansas team.

Community Impact

Support events, workshops, or open-source contributions that grow local talent.

Micro-grant recipients commit to a short public update and a 30-minute share-out with the community.

Funding FAQ

Can I apply if I'm outside Arkansas?

Scholarships prioritize Arkansas residents or students. Micro-grants require a project that benefits Arkansas communities.

Can I reapply if I'm not selected?

Yes. Share what changed in your project since the last cycle and reapply in the next window.

How are funds distributed?

We send funds via ACH or check within two weeks of award notification.
